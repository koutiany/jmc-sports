Reflection on week 2

Week 2: What of the stand out points of the video was about how meaningful the Brazilian World Cup meant to everyone in Brazil. It must be said though that the video was done by FIFA as part of their promotion program. This is meaningful because they obviously want the World Cup to be as hyped up as possible and this is done by making everyone feel amped to watch their team. Because this video is staging the event this way it is worth considering what the actual attitude of the nation is like towards the team. I think when considering the rural villagers and how they were in tune with the team and looking forward to them competing that it is infact a unifying event. There is definitely inequality when it comes to the background of the players on the team because for many, it comes from years of being apart of expensive club teams. The best teams often matured from a young age with the help of their parents who have the means and the time to enroll their children. It calls into question how people in the countryside who are not afforded these kinds of opportunities see themselves in the greater picture of Brazilian soccer. I think soccer itself is unifying enough because it is super fun to watch people born in the same area as you take their skills to the global spectacle. Watching these players be better or worse than other players from different countries is a source of national pride because sport creates an event playingfield where the better talent wins out. I think people can identity with this because while they are not afforded the same kinds of opportunities as others, a place where these things can be thrown aside and becomes a space where purpose is exposed.

The Goal of this project

Through the discussions, students examine how sports were created and utilized in the nation-building process from both historical and contemporary perspectives. Students are encouraged to think about the following questions: Why do people practice sports? What roles do sports play in your daily life? How do different parties (founding figures, professional organizations, athletes, spectators, sponsors, media, policymakers, etc.) interact with sports? How have national, regional, racial, and gender identities been presented in sports?

This honors option also provides training in creating digital content and project management. Students are anticipated to participate in building an online website where they document their reflection on the relationship between sports and society.
